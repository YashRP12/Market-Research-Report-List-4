<p><p>Belt filters are commonly classified into two main types: Horizontal Belt Filters and Vertical Belt Filters. Horizontal Belt Filters are designed to operate horizontally and are suitable for continuous processing of slurries with high solid content. Vertical Belt Filters, on the other hand, are designed to operate vertically and are typically used for thickening and dewatering processes. Both types of belt filters are used in industries such as mining, chemical processing, and wastewater treatment to efficiently separate solids from liquids.</p></p>
