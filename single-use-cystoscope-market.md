<p><p>Single Use Cystoscopes refer to disposable medical devices used for visualizing the inside of the bladder and urethra. There are two main types available in the market: Flexible Single Use Cystoscopes and Rigid Single Use Cystoscopes. Flexible cystoscopes are more versatile and can navigate bends and curves more easily, while rigid cystoscopes provide clearer images but are less flexible in their movement. Both types offer the advantage of being disposable, reducing the risk of cross-contamination and improving patient safety.</p></p>
