<p><p>Temperature sensors RTD market types include 2-wire, 3-wire, 4-wire, and others. 2-wire RTD sensors have the simplest wiring configuration, suitable for short distances and low-cost applications. 3-wire RTD sensors provide better accuracy by compensating for lead wire resistance. 4-wire RTD sensors offer the highest accuracy by eliminating lead wire resistance. Other market types may include wireless RTD sensors, self-heated RTD sensors, or specialized RTD sensors for unique applications. Each type offers different advantages in terms of accuracy, cost, and application suitability.</p></p>
