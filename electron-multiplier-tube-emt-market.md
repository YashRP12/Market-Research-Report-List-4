<p><p>Electron Multiplier Tube (EMT) market can be categorized into three main types: Discrete Dynode Electron Multipliers, Continuous Dynode Electron Multipliers, and Others. Discrete Dynode Electron Multipliers consist of individual dynodes separated by insulating materials, providing efficient amplification of electron signals. Continuous Dynode Electron Multipliers, on the other hand, have a continuous dynode structure that allows for higher signal detection. The Others category includes any electron multiplier tubes that do not fit into the aforementioned types, offering a diverse range of options for different applications.</p></p>
