<p><ul><li>Centralised</li><li>Decentralised</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centralised hot water solutions refer to systems where hot water is generated in a central location and distributed to multiple end-users through a network of pipes. This type of market is common in large buildings or complexes where a single system serves the entire property. </p><p>Decentralised hot water solutions, on the other hand, involve individual hot water heaters located within each unit or area of a building. This setup allows for more control and customization for each user, but may require more maintenance and energy usage compared to centralised systems.</p></p>
