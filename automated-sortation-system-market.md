<p><p>Automated Sortation System Market consists of two main types: Linear Sortation Systems and Loop Sortation Systems. Linear Sortation Systems utilize conveyor belts to transport items in a straight line, while Loop Sortation Systems use a looped track to continuously circulate items. Linear systems are best suited for facilities with limited space, while Loop systems are ideal for high-volume operations. Both systems employ sensors and software to accurately sort and route items to their designated destination, improving efficiency and productivity in various industries.</p></p>
