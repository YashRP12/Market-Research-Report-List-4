<p><p>A colostomy bag, also known as an ostomy bag, is a medical device used to collect waste from the colon after a colostomy procedure. It is a pouch that is attached to the stoma, an opening in the abdomen created surgically, allowing waste to be collected and disposed of properly. Colostomy bags are essential for individuals who have undergone colostomy surgery due to conditions such as bowel cancer, inflammatory bowel disease, or diverticulitis.</p><p>The global Colostomy Bags Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.30% during the forecast period.
