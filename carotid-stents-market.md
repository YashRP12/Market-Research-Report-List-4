<p><p>Carotid stents market consists of two main types - Balloon Expandable Stents and Self-Expanding Stents. Balloon Expandable Stents are inserted into the carotid artery and are inflated with a balloon to hold the artery open. On the other hand, Self-Expanding Stents are designed to expand on their own once inserted into the artery. Both types of stents are used to treat carotid artery stenosis, a condition where the carotid arteries narrow and restrict blood flow to the brain.</p></p>
