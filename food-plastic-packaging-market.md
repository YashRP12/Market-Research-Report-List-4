<p><p>Food plastic packaging market can be categorized into three main types based on the type of polymers used: thermoset polymer, thermoplastic polymer, and elastomer. Thermoset polymer packaging materials are rigid and non-recyclable, providing strong and durable packaging solutions. Thermoplastic polymer packaging materials are flexible and recyclable, easily molded into different shapes for a wide range of food products. Elastomer packaging materials are elastic and durable, offering a seal-tight and stretchable packaging option for various food items.</p></p>
