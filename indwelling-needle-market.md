<p><ul><li>Open Type</li><li>Closed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Indwelling needle market can be divided into two types: open type and closed type. Open type indwelling needles have a port that remains open and allows for continuous infusion or withdrawal of fluids without the need for needle removal. Closed type indwelling needles, on the other hand, have a septum that seals the needle after each use, reducing the risk of contamination. Both types have their own advantages and are used based on the specific requirements of the medical procedure.</p></p>
