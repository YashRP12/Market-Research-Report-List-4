<p><p>Ostomy care products and accessories are essential for individuals with colostomies, ileostomies, and urostomies, as they help manage waste elimination and promote hygiene. Colostomy products cater to individuals who have undergone a surgical procedure diverting the colon to the abdominal wall, while ileostomy products are designed for those with an ileostomy, where the small intestine is redirected. Urostomy products, on the other hand, are used by individuals who have had surgery rerouting urine through an opening in the abdomen. These products ensure comfort, security, and ease of use for ostomy patients.</p></p>
