<p><p>Booster pump market consists of two main types: Single Stage and Multiple Stage pumps. Single Stage pumps are designed with only one impeller and are suitable for applications where a moderate increase in pressure is required. On the other hand, Multiple Stage pumps have multiple impellers stacked together to provide higher pressure outputs. These pumps are ideal for applications that require significantly higher pressures. Both types of pumps play a crucial role in various industries like water treatment, irrigation, and oil & gas.</p></p>
