<p><p>Closed Loop Stepper Motors are classified into three main types: Permanent Magnet, Hybrid, and Variable Reluctance. Permanent Magnet stepper motors use a permanent magnet rotor and have high torque capabilities. Hybrid stepper motors combine features of both permanent magnet and variable reluctance motors, offering high performance and precision. Variable Reluctance stepper motors rely on the variable reluctance principle for operation and are known for their simplicity and low cost. Each type has its unique characteristics and advantages, catering to different applications in the market.</p></p>
