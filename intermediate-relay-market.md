<p><strong>The Intermediate Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Type</li><li>Electromagnetic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intermediate relays are used to control the flow of electric current in various circuits. Static type relays use semiconductor devices like transistors and diodes to control the current flow. On the other hand, electromagnetic relays use electromagnetic coils to open or close the contacts. Static type relays are known for their high reliability and durability, while electromagnetic relays are preferred for their versatility and ability to handle high currents. Both types of relays are commonly used in industrial automation, power distribution, and control systems.</p></p>
