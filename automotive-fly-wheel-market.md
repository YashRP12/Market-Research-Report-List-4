<p><ul><li>Single mass flywheel</li><li>Dual mass flywheel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive flywheel market consists of two main types: single mass flywheel and dual mass flywheel. Single mass flywheels are a simpler and more traditional design, typically found in older vehicles. They offer a more direct connection between the engine and transmission, providing better responsiveness and durability. On the other hand, dual mass flywheels are a more complex design that offers smoother and quieter operation by using a dampening mechanism to absorb engine vibrations. They are commonly found in modern vehicles to improve overall driving comfort and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
