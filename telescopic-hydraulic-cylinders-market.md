<p><p>Telescopic Hydraulic Cylinders are available in two main types: Single Acting Cylinders and Double Acting Cylinders. Single Acting Cylinders exert force in one direction only, typically using gravity or an external force to retract the piston. Double Acting Cylinders, on the other hand, use hydraulic pressure to both extend and retract the piston, providing more control over the movement and force applied. Both types play a crucial role in various industries such as construction, agriculture, and manufacturing.</p></p>
