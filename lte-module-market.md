<p><p>LTE Module Market includes EPC (Evolved Packet Core) and ENode B (Evolved Node B) types. EPC is the core network of LTE, responsible for functions like user authentication, mobility management, and policy enforcement. ENode B is the base station in LTE, connecting user devices to the core network. The EPC market focuses on network infrastructure and services, while the ENode B market is centered around base stations and network coverage. Both are crucial components in providing high-speed mobile internet services.</p></p>
