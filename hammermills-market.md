<p><p>Hammermills consist of two main market types - "Up Running" and "Down Running" Hammer Mills. </p><p>The "Up Running" Hammer Mill operates with the rotor spinning in the opposite direction to the material feed, creating high impact and efficient size reduction. </p><p>On the other hand, the "Down Running" Hammer Mill features a rotor that spins in the same direction as the material feed, offering a more gentle and controlled reduction of particle size. </p><p>Each type caters to different processing needs and applications in industries such as agriculture, mining, and recycling.</p></p>
