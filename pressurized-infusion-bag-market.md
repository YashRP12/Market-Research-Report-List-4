<p><p>The Pressurized Infusion Bag Market offers various types including Airbag, Bungee Band, Liquid Bag Fixed Film, Trachea, Valve, and Inflatable Ball. Airbag infusion bags utilize air pressure to administer fluids, while Bungee Band bags use elastic bands. Liquid Bag Fixed Film bags have a fixed film for fluid infusion, and Trachea bags have a tube for insertion. Valve bags have a pressure-release valve, and Inflatable Ball bags use a ball mechanism for pressurization. Each type offers unique features for specific medical applications.</p></p>
