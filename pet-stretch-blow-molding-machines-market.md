<p><p>PET Stretch Blow Molding Machines are used to produce PET bottles by stretching and blowing molten PET into a mold. The market is divided into Single Step and Two Step machines. Single Step machines perform both preform heating and bottle forming in one step, making them faster but less flexible. Two Step machines separate preform making and bottle forming, allowing for more customization and control over the final product. Both types have their own advantages and cater to different production needs.</p></p>
