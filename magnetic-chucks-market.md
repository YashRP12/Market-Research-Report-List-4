<p><p>Magnetic chucks are used in machining and manufacturing processes for holding ferromagnetic materials securely in place during operations. There are three main types of magnetic chucks: Ordinary Rectangular Chuck, which is commonly used for general machining purposes; Strong Magnetic Chuck, which provides a stronger holding force for heavier or larger workpieces; and Dense Magnetic Chuck, which offers a high density of magnetic poles for enhanced stability and precision. Each type serves specific purposes depending on the size and weight of the workpiece being machined.</p></p>
