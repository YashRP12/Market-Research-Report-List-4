<p><p>Back grinding tape is used in the semiconductor industry for thinning wafers. There are two main types of back grinding tapes: UV curable and non-UV curable. UV curable tapes require exposure to ultraviolet light for adhesion, while non-UV curable tapes use other methods for adhesion. UV curable tapes are commonly used for their fast curing time and strong bond, while non-UV curable tapes are preferred for their compatibility with sensitive materials. Both types play a crucial role in the back grinding process for semiconductor production.</p></p>
