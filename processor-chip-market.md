<p><p>Processor chip market can be categorized into three main types: Digital Chip, Analog Chip, and Hybrid Chip. Digital Chips are used for processing binary data and performing complex calculations in applications such as computers and smartphones. Analog Chips process continuous data such as sound, light, and temperature in devices like sensors and medical equipment. Hybrid Chips combine the features of both digital and analog chips, providing the benefits of both types in applications that require mixed-signal processing capabilities.</p></p>
