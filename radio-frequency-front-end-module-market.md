<p><p>The Radio Frequency Front End Module market includes various types of components such as Power Amplifiers (PA), RF Switches, Low Noise Amplifiers (LNA), RF Filters, and Others. Power Amplifiers are used to amplify signals, RF Switches are used to switch between different signals, Low Noise Amplifiers reduce noise in signals, RF Filters are used to filter out unwanted frequencies, and other components include mixers, oscillators, and demodulators. Each type plays a crucial role in the functioning of RF front end modules.</p></p>
