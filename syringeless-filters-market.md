<p><p>Syringeless filters are commonly available in two main market types based on the pore size: 0.2 µm and 0.45 µm. The 0.2 µm filters are designed to remove smaller particles and microorganisms, making them suitable for sterilizing or clarifying solutions in laboratories and pharmaceutical industries. On the other hand, the 0.45 µm filters are used for general filtration applications where smaller particles are not a concern. Both types of filters offer a convenient and efficient way to process samples without the need for a syringe.</p></p>
