<p><p>Gas density sensors market can be categorized into two main types: point sensor and open path sensor. Point sensors are designed to measure gas density at a specific point in a controlled environment, typically used for indoor air quality monitoring or leak detection. On the other hand, open path sensors can cover a larger area and detect gas density along a path, commonly used for outdoor air quality monitoring or perimeter monitoring. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring safety and environmental protection in various industries.</p></p>
