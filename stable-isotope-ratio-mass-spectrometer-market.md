<p><p>Stable Isotope Ratio Mass Spectrometer (IRMS) market is categorized into different types based on the techniques used for analysis. Gas Chromatography-IRMS (GC-IRMS), Element Analyzer-IRMS (EA-IRMS), Liquid Chromatography-IRMS (LC-IRMS), and other specialized methods. GC-IRMS measures isotopic ratios in gas samples, EA-IRMS is used for solid samples, and LC-IRMS analyzes isotopic composition in liquid samples. Other methods include thermal Ionization-IRMS, Pyrolysis-IRMS, and Cavity Ringdown Spectroscopy. Each type of IRMS offers specific advantages for different types of samples and applications.</p></p>
