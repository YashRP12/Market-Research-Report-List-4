<p><p>Kapton heaters are available in two main types: rectangle type and round type. Rectangle type Kapton heaters are suitable for applications requiring a more uniform distribution of heat, as they cover a larger surface area. On the other hand, round type Kapton heaters are ideal for applications where space is limited or where a specific point needs to be heated. Both types offer excellent flexibility and durability, making them popular choices in industries such as aerospace, electronics, and medical devices.</p></p>
