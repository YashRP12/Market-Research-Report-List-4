<p><p>Monolithic cleaning equipment refers to cleaning systems specifically designed for cleaning individual silicon wafers. These systems are typically used in semiconductor manufacturing facilities where precision cleaning is required. On the other hand, tank cleaning equipment is used for cleaning larger batches of silicon wafers in tanks or chambers. This equipment is more suitable for high-volume production environments where multiple wafers need to be cleaned simultaneously. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in maintaining the cleanliness and quality of silicon wafers used in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
