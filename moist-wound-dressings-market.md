Foam dressings absorb excess exudate, hydrocolloids provide a protective barrier, alginates promote hemostasis, antimicrobial dressings help prevent infection, transparent films allow for easy wound monitoring, and hydrogels provide hydration to the wound bed. Each type of dressing serves a specific purpose in wound care management.</p></p>
