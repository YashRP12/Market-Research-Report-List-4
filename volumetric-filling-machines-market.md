<p><p>Volumetric filling machines are used for accurately filling containers with liquid or semi-liquid products. There are two main types of volumetric filling machines: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic machines operate independently and can fill multiple containers simultaneously, making them ideal for high-volume production environments. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention, such as placing containers under the filling nozzles, but still offer precise and efficient filling capabilities. Both types of machines are widely used in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.</p></p>
