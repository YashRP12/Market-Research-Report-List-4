<p><p>Metamaterials for antenna market can be categorized into various types, including Negative-index Metamaterials (NIM), Single Negative (SNG) Metamaterials, Hyperbolic Metamaterials (HMMs), and others. Negative-index Metamaterials have unique properties that allow for the manipulation and control of electromagnetic waves. Single Negative Metamaterials exhibit negative permittivity or negative permeability. Hyperbolic Metamaterials have hyperbolic dispersion, enabling the focusing of light beyond diffraction limits. Other types of metamaterials in the market offer different functionalities and advantages for antenna applications.</p></p>
