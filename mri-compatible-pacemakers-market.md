<p><p>MRI compatible pacemakers are medical devices specially designed to be safe for use in magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) environments. <p><p>MRI compatible pacemakers are classified into three types based on their functionality: single-chamber pacemakers, dual-chamber pacemakers, and biventricular/CRT pacemakers. Single-chamber pacemakers regulate the rhythm of one chamber of the heart, while dual-chamber pacemakers control the rhythm of two chambers. Biventricular/CRT pacemakers coordinate contractions between the two ventricles for improved heart function. Each type offers specific benefits depending on the patient's condition and needs, and they are all designed to be compatible with MRI scans to ensure accurate diagnosis and treatment.</p></p>
