<p><strong>The Emergency Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-contained Power Supply</li><li>Centralized Power Supply</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Emergency lighting market can be divided into two main types: self-contained power supply and centralized power supply. In self-contained power supply systems, each individual light fitting has its own battery backup, ensuring that each light continues to function independently in the event of a power outage. In centralized power supply systems, all lights are connected to a central battery backup, providing power to multiple light fittings. Both types of systems are designed to ensure that emergency lighting remains operational during power failures, helping to guide people to safety.</p></p>
