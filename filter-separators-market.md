<p><p>Filter separators are devices used to remove solids, liquids, and gases from a stream of mixed substances. They can be classified into two main types based on their orientation: vertical and horizontal. Vertical filter separators are designed to handle high liquid loads and are suitable for applications where gravity plays a significant role in separation. On the other hand, horizontal filter separators are better suited for gas-dominant streams and are typically used in high-pressure applications where space is limited.</p></p>
