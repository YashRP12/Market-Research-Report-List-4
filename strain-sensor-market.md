<p><p>Strain sensors are devices used to measure deformation or strain in an object or material. There are various types of strain sensors, including surface strain sensors, transverse strain sensors, and longitudinal strain sensors. Surface strain sensors measure strain on the surface of an object, transverse strain sensors measure strain perpendicular to an object's surface, and longitudinal strain sensors measure strain parallel to an object's surface. Each type of sensor is designed to accurately measure strain in specific directions, allowing for precise and reliable data collection in various applications.</p></p>
