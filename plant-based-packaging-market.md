<p><p>Plant-based packaging can be categorized into two main types: flexible packaging and rigid packaging. Flexible packaging includes products such as pouches, bags, and wraps that are made from plant-based materials like cellulose or corn starch. This type of packaging is lightweight and versatile, making it suitable for a wide range of products. On the other hand, rigid packaging includes containers, bottles, and trays made from plant-based plastics or fibers. These packaging types are more durable and provide better protection for the products inside.</p></p>
