<p><p>Handheld acoustic cameras are primarily divided into two types: 2D microphone arrays and 3D microphone arrays. </p><p>2D microphone arrays capture sound data in a two-dimensional plane, providing a comprehensive view of sound sources within a certain area. On the other hand, 3D microphone arrays capture sound data in three dimensions, allowing for more precise localization of sound sources in terms of both direction and distance. </p><p>These technologies are crucial in various industries, such as aerospace, automotive, and environmental monitoring, for sound source identification and analysis.</p></p>
