<p><p>Stage illumination market is dominated by three main types of lighting sources: LED, halogen, and discharge. LED lighting has gained popularity due to its energy efficiency, long lifespan, and ability to produce a wide range of colors. Halogen lighting is known for its bright and warm light output, but is less energy efficient compared to LED. Discharge lighting, which includes sodium vapor and metal halide lamps, provides intense and high-quality light output, making it suitable for large venues and outdoor events. Each type of lighting source has its own advantages and is used in different settings based on the specific lighting requirements.</p></p>
