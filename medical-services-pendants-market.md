<p><p>Medical services pendants are categorized into two main types: single arm pendant systems and multi arm pendant systems. Single arm pendant systems consist of a single arm that is attached to the ceiling, allowing for the hanging of medical equipment such as monitors and IV bags. On the other hand, multi arm pendant systems have multiple arms that can be adjusted and positioned independently, providing more flexibility and space for medical equipment. Both types of pendant systems are commonly used in hospitals and healthcare facilities to efficiently organize and access medical devices during patient care.</p></p>
