<p><p>Refrigeration compressor market includes various types such as reciprocating compressors, rotary compressors, screw compressors, and others. Reciprocating compressors use a piston to compress the refrigerant, rotary compressors use rotating impellers to compress the gas, and screw compressors use interlocking screws to compress the gas. Other types of compressors may include centrifugal compressors or scroll compressors. Each type offers unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different applications and industries within the refrigeration market.</p></p>
