<p><p>Offshore oil skimming systems are used to clean up oil spills in the ocean. There are three main types of these systems: belt oil skimmers, disk oil skimmers, and others. Belt oil skimmers use a continuous belt to remove oil from water, while disk oil skimmers use rotating disks to collect oil. The other types of oil skimmers may include weir skimmers, brush skimmers, or drum skimmers, each offering unique features and benefits for oil spill cleanup in offshore environments.</p></p>
