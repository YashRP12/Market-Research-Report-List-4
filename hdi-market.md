<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The HDI Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HDI PCB (1+N+1)</li><li>HDI PCB (2+N+2)</li><li>ELIC (Every Layer Interconnection)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HDI PCB (1+N+1) refers to a type of high-density interconnect printed circuit board with one buildup core and one buildup layer on each side of the core. HDI PCB (2+N+2) has two buildup layers on each side of the core. ELIC (Every Layer Interconnection) market involves creating interconnections between every layer of the PCB, allowing for higher density and more complex designs. These market types cater to the demand for compact electronic devices with increased functionality and performance.</p></p>
