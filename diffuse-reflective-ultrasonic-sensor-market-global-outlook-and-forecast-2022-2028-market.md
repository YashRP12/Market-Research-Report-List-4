<p><p>Diffuse reflective ultrasonic sensors are devices that use sound waves to detect the presence or distance of objects. The global market for these sensors is segmented into two types: analog output and digital output. Analog output sensors provide a continuous signal proportional to the distance of the object, while digital output sensors provide a discrete signal indicating the presence or absence of an object. Both types of sensors are widely used in various industries such as automotive, manufacturing, and robotics for proximity sensing and object detection applications.</p></p>
