<p><strong>The Bicycle Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Loose Ball Bearings</li><li>Retainer Bearings</li><li>Sealed Cartridge Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bicycle bearings can be categorized into three main types: Loose ball bearings, retainer bearings, and sealed cartridge bearings. Loose ball bearings are traditional bearings consisting of separate balls housed within a bearing race. Retainer bearings have balls held in place by a retainer or cage for easier installation and maintenance. Sealed cartridge bearings are fully enclosed units with seals to protect against dirt and water, offering longer life and less maintenance. Each type offers advantages and is suitable for different riding conditions.</p></p>
