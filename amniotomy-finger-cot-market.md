<p><p>Amniotomy finger cots are used during the procedure of breaking the amniotic sac to induce labor. They are available in two main types: latex and non-latex. Latex finger cots are made from natural rubber latex, while non-latex finger cots are made from synthetic materials like nitrile or vinyl. Both types offer protection against infection during the procedure and provide a barrier between the healthcare provider's finger and the amniotic sac. The choice between latex and non-latex finger cots depends on any allergies or sensitivities the patient may have.</p></p>
