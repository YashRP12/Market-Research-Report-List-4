<p><p>Active Epoxy Toughening Agents are classified into two main types: Rubber Toughening Agents and Thermoplastic Resin Toughening Agents. Rubber Toughening Agents are rubber-like materials that are added to epoxy resins to improve impact resistance and flexibility. Thermoplastic Resin Toughening Agents are thermoplastic resins that are added to epoxy resins to enhance toughness and durability. Both types of toughening agents play a crucial role in improving the mechanical properties of epoxy resins and are widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
