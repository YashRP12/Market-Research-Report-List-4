<p><p>GPS tracking devices market can be categorized into three main types: Standalone Tracker, OBD Device, and Advance Tracker. Standalone trackers are small, portable devices that can be placed discreetly in vehicles or items for real-time tracking. OBD devices plug into a car's OBD-II port and provide vehicle diagnostics along with location tracking. Advance trackers offer additional features such as geo-fencing, fuel monitoring, and driver behavior analysis. Each type caters to specific needs and preferences of users in the GPS tracking market.</p></p>
