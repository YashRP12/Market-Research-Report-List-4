<p><strong>The Water Mist Sprinkler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Pipe System</li><li>Dry Pipe System</li><li>Deluge System</li><li>Preaction System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Water Mist Sprinkler Market consists of various types based on the system design. Wet Pipe System utilizes pipes filled with water that discharge immediately when triggered. Dry Pipe System prevents freezing by holding pressurized air in the pipes until activation. Deluge System releases water in a flood-like manner for high hazard areas. Preaction System combines aspects of dry and wet pipe systems by requiring two triggers before water is released, suitable for areas with false alarm concerns.</p></p>
