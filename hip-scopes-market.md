<p><ul><li>Pincer Type</li><li>Cam Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pincer Type and Cam Type are two common market types within the field of hip scopes. Pincer Type involves an over-coverage of the hip socket, leading to impingement of the hip joint. On the other hand, Cam Type refers to a bone growth on the surface of the femoral head that can cause friction and limited range of motion. Both market types are important considerations in diagnosing and treating hip joint abnormalities, and may require surgical intervention such as hip arthroscopy.</p></p>
