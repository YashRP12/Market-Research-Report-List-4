<p><p>Solar street lights come in two main types - standalone and grid connected. </p><p>Standalone solar street lights are individual units that operate independently, using batteries to store energy collected during the day to illuminate the streets at night. They are ideal for remote areas with limited access to the electrical grid.</p><p>Grid connected solar street lights, on the other hand, are connected to the main electrical grid. They can draw power from the grid when the solar energy is insufficient, providing a reliable lighting solution for urban areas.</p></p>
