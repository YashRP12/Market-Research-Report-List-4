<p><strong>The Touch Type Nitrogen Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Colorimetric Method</li><li>Electrode Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Touch Type Nitrogen Analyzers can be categorized into two main types: Colorimetric Method and Electrode Method. Colorimetric Method analyzers measure nitrogen content by detecting color changes in a reaction caused by nitrogen presence. This method is commonly used in laboratories for accurate measurements. On the other hand, Electrode Method analyzers measure nitrogen levels through an electrochemical process. This method is often used in field testing due to its portability and quick results. Both methods offer accurate and reliable nitrogen analysis for various applications.</p></p>
