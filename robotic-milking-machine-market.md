<p><p>Robotic milking machines come in three main market types: standalone units, multiple stall units, and rotary units. Standalone units are designed for single cow milking at a time, offering flexibility and ease of use. Multiple stall units can handle multiple cows simultaneously, increasing efficiency on larger dairy farms. Rotary units are the largest and most advanced, with a rotating platform that allows cows to enter and exit for milking in a continuous process. Each market type caters to different farm sizes and milking needs.</p></p>
