<p><p>Mechanical Impurity Tester is a device used to measure the amount of impurities in various liquids. Two common types of markets for these testers are Weight Method and Membrane Filtration. In Weight Method market, the impurities are measured by weighing the residue left after evaporation of the liquid sample. In Membrane Filtration market, the impurities are captured on a membrane filter, allowing for easy visual inspection and analysis. Both methods offer reliable and accurate results for detecting mechanical impurities in liquids.</p></p>
