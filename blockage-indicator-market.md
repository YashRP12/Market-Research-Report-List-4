<p><p>Blockage Indicator Market consists of different types such as Back Pressure Indicator, Differential Pressure Indicator, and Others. Back Pressure Indicator measures the pressure buildup at the outlet, indicating a potential blockage. Differential Pressure Indicator measures the pressure difference between two points in a system, detecting blockages. Other types of indicators may include visual or audible alarms, temperature sensors, or flow rate monitors to identify blockages in various systems. These indicators help ensure proper functioning and prevent damage in industrial processes.</p></p>
