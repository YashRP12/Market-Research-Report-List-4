<p><p>Vehicle anti-theft systems are crucial for safeguarding vehicles against theft. Alarm systems detect unauthorized entry or tampering with the vehicle. Immobilizers prevent the engine from starting without the correct key or code. Steering lock systems prevent the steering wheel from being turned without the correct key. Passive keyless entry allows the driver to unlock and start the vehicle without physically using the key. Central locking systems lock and unlock all doors simultaneously. Biometric capture devices use unique physical traits like fingerprints for vehicle access control.</p></p>
