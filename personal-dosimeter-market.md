Direct read electronic dosimeters provide real-time measurement of radiation levels and can immediately alert individuals of any potential danger. Both types of dosimeters are essential tools in ensuring the safety of workers in nuclear power plants, medical facilities, and other radiation-prone environments.</p></p>
