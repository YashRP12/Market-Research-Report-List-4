<p><p>Optical Fiber Market Types include Single Mode Optical Fiber and Multimode Optical Fiber. Single Mode Optical Fiber is designed for long-distance, high-speed data transmission with a single pathway for light to travel through. It is ideal for telecommunications and networking applications. On the other hand, Multimode Optical Fiber allows multiple light modes to travel simultaneously, making it suitable for shorter distances and lower bandwidth requirements, such as in local area networks. Both types cater to different needs and applications in the evolving fiber optic industry.</p></p>
