Electronic PCA pumps are programmable devices that allow patients to self-administer pain medication at regular intervals. Mechanical PCA pumps, on the other hand, rely on manual operation by the patient to deliver medication. Both types of PCA pumps help in managing pain effectively and ensuring patients receive the necessary medication without the need for constant nursing assistance.</p></p>
