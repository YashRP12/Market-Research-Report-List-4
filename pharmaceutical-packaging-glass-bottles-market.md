<p><p>Pharmaceutical packaging glass bottles are available in three main types: regular soda-lime glass, treated soda-lime glass, and borosilicate glass. Regular soda-lime glass is the most common type and is used for general packaging applications. Treated soda-lime glass has added chemical treatments to enhance its strength and durability. Borosilicate glass is known for its superior resistance to thermal shock and chemical corrosion, making it suitable for storing sensitive medications. Each type of glass bottle caters to specific pharmaceutical packaging needs based on their properties and characteristics.</p></p>
