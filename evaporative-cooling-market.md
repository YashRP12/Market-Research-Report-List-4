<p><p>Evaporative cooling is a method of cooling air through the evaporation of water. There are three main types of evaporative cooling markets: Direct Evaporative Cooling, where water is in direct contact with air to cool it down; Indirect Evaporative Cooling, which uses a heat exchanger to cool the air without adding humidity; and Two-stage Evaporative Cooling, which combines both direct and indirect methods for more efficient cooling. Each type has its own advantages and is used in various applications based on specific needs.</p></p>
