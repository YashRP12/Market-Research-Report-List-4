<p><p>Injectable pain medication market includes two main types: Nonsteroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs) and Central Analgesics. NSAIDs reduce pain by blocking enzymes involved in inflammation and pain signaling. They are commonly used for mild to moderate pain relief. Central Analgesics act on the central nervous system to alter pain perception, making them suitable for moderate to severe pain management. Both types of medications are administered through injections for quicker and more effective pain relief in conditions such as post-operative pain, cancer-related pain, and chronic pain.</p></p>
