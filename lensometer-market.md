<p><p>Manual lensometers require the user to manually align and focus the lens before obtaining readings, while automatic lensometers perform these tasks automatically. Manual lensometers are typically more affordable and portable, making them suitable for smaller practices or mobile screenings. On the other hand, automatic lensometers offer quicker and more precise measurements, making them ideal for high-volume practices or clinics. Both types of lensometers are essential tools in optometry and ophthalmology for accurately measuring the prescription of eyeglasses.</p></p>
