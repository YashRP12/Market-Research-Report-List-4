One of the key factors driving the growth of this market is the increasing awareness about the health and environmental benefits of plant-based diets. Consumers are becoming more conscious of the impact of meat consumption on their health and the environment, leading to a rise in demand for imitation meat products.</p><p>In addition, the growing adoption of vegetarian and flexitarian lifestyles, along with advancements in food technology, are also contributing to the market's growth.
