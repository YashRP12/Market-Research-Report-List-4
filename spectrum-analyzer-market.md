<p><p>Spectrum analyzers are electronic tools used to measure and analyze frequency signals. There are three main types of spectrum analyzers in the market: Swept-Tuned, Vector Signal, and Real-Time analyzers. Swept-Tuned analyzers scan a specific range of frequencies systematically. Vector Signal analyzers are capable of analyzing both amplitude and phase information simultaneously. Real-Time analyzers provide instant and continuous monitoring of signals. Each type caters to different needs and requirements in various industries such as telecommunications, electronics, and research.</p></p>
