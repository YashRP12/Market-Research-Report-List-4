<p><p>Inductive absolute encoders can be classified into two main types: single-turn and multi-turn. Single-turn encoders provide position information within one full revolution, while multi-turn encoders offer position data over multiple revolutions. Single-turn encoders are suitable for applications requiring precision within one revolution, while multi-turn encoders are ideal for systems needing continuous position tracking over multiple revolutions. Each type has its unique advantages and is selected based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
