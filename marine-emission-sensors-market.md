<p><ul><li>Extractive CEMs</li><li>In-Situ CEMs</li><li>Portable CEMs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine Emission Sensors Market comprises three main types: Extractive Continuous Emission Monitors (CEMs), In-Situ CEMs, and Portable CEMs. Extractive CEMs extract a sample of the gas stream to analyze emissions. In-Situ CEMs measure emissions directly within the combustion process, providing real-time data. Portable CEMs are small, handheld devices that can be easily transported to different locations for temporary emission monitoring. Each type offers unique advantages in monitoring marine emissions, catering to different needs and requirements of the marine industry.</p></p>
