<p><strong>The Meso-Micro Porous Carbon Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microporous Materials (Less than 2 nm)</li><li>Mesoporous Materials (2-50 nm)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Meso-Micro Porous Carbon Materials refer to materials with both mesoporous and microporous structure. Microporous materials have pores with a size of less than 2 nm, allowing for the adsorption of small molecules. Mesoporous materials have pores ranging from 2 to 50 nm, providing a larger surface area for adsorption and catalysis. These materials are widely used in applications such as gas storage, water purification, and energy storage due to their unique pore structure and high surface area.</p></p>
