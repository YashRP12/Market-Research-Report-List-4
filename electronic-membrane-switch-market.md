<p><p>An Electronic Membrane Switch is a low-profile, flexible electrical device that uses pressure to open or close a circuit. It consists of a printed circuit board (PCB) with a thin, flexible overlay that is composed of multiple layers of materials such as polyester or polycarbonate. When pressure is applied to the overlay, it activates the switch by making contact with the underlying circuit.</p><p>The Electronic Membrane Switch Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.60% during the forecast period.
