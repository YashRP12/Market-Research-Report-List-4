<p><ul><li>Acrylic</li><li>Epoxy</li><li>Polyurethane</li><li>Alkyd</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water-based resins are commonly divided into four main types: acrylic, epoxy, polyurethane, and alkyd. Acrylic resins are known for their excellent weather resistance and color retention. Epoxy resins are highly durable and offer strong adhesion properties. Polyurethane resins are often used for their abrasion resistance and flexibility. Alkyd resins are valued for their fast drying time and excellent leveling properties. Each type of resin has specific characteristics that make it suitable for different applications in industries such as construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
