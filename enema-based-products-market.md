<p><p>Enema based products can be categorized into four main types of markets: Cleansing, Carminative, Retention, and Return-Flow. Cleansing enemas are designed to help eliminate waste material from the colon. Carminative enemas help reduce gas and bloating in the digestive system. Retention enemas involve holding the solution in the colon for a period of time to achieve a specific therapeutic effect. Return-Flow enemas involve introducing a solution into the colon and then expelling it along with any waste material. Each type of enema serves a unique purpose in promoting digestive health.</p></p>
