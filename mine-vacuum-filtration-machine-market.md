<p><p>The mine vacuum filtration machine market includes various types such as the cylindrical (drum) filter, disc filter, and horizontal belt filter. Cylindrical filters consist of rotating drums that separate solid particles from liquids. Disc filters use a series of rotating discs to capture solids. Horizontal belt filters use a continuous belt to extract solids from liquids. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in different mining operations based on factors such as particle size, flow rate, and space limitations.</p></p>
