<p><p>Semi-submersible drilling platforms can be classified into two main types based on their underwater float design: underwater float types and caisson types. Underwater float types rely on buoyancy to stay afloat, while caisson types use hollow cylindrical structures for stability. Both types offer different advantages in terms of stability, maneuverability, and operational efficiency. Companies in the offshore drilling industry may choose between these two market types depending on their specific requirements and project needs.</p></p>
