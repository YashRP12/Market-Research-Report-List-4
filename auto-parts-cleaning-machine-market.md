<p><ul><li>Water-based Cleaning Machine</li><li>Solvent-based Cleaning Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Auto Parts Cleaning Machines are divided into two main types based on the cleaning solution they use: Water-based and Solvent-based. Water-based cleaning machines use water mixed with detergents to clean auto parts, making them more environmentally friendly and cost-effective. On the other hand, Solvent-based cleaning machines use solvents like mineral spirits or hydrocarbons to effectively remove grease and dirt from auto parts. While solvent-based machines may be more powerful, they are also more expensive and can pose environmental and health risks.</p></p>
