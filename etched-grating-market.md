<p><strong>The Etched Grating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission Grating</li><li>Reflection Grating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Etched Grating Market consists of two main types - Transmission Grating and Reflection Grating. Transmission Grating allows light to pass through the grating, separating the spectrum into its individual components. On the other hand, Reflection Grating reflects light, diffracting it into specific wavelengths. Both types of gratings are widely used in various applications such as spectroscopy, telecommunications, and aerospace. The choice between the two types depends on the specific needs of the application, such as resolution, efficiency, and spectral range.</p></p>
