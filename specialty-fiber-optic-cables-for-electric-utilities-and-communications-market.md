<p><p>Specialty fiber optic cables for electric utilities and communications are designed for specific applications within these industries. OPGW (Optical Ground Wire) cables are used for transmitting data and power on overhead transmission lines. OPPC (Optical Phase Conductor) cables are ideal for delivering high-speed data and power in high-voltage environments. ADSS (All-Dielectric Self-Supporting) cables offer increased durability and flexibility for aerial installations. OPLC (Optical Power Line Communication) cables combine power transmission and communication capabilities, providing a cost-effective solution for smart grid applications.</p></p>
