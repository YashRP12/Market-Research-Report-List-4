<p><p>Non-network connected non-IC card power smart meters come in two main market types: simplex and three-phase. </p><p>Simplex meters are designed for single-phase electrical systems, commonly found in residential buildings. They measure the energy consumption of a single electrical circuit.</p><p>Three-phase meters, on the other hand, are used for larger commercial or industrial settings with three-phase electrical systems. They measure the energy consumption of all three phases separately, providing a more detailed analysis of energy usage.</p></p>
