<p><p>Dental milling tools are categorized into two main types: Horizontal type and Vertical type. Horizontal type milling tools have a spindle that is parallel to the ground, allowing for precise and efficient milling of dental materials. Vertical type milling tools, on the other hand, have a spindle that is perpendicular to the ground, offering versatility in milling different angles and shapes. Both types of tools are essential in the dental industry for creating high-quality dental prosthetics and appliances.</p></p>
