<p><p>Steam meter market types include Vortex Flowmeters, Differential-pressure Flowmeters, and Others. Vortex Flowmeters measure steam flow by detecting vortices shed by a bluff body within the flow stream. Differential-pressure Flowmeters determine steam flow by measuring the pressure drop across a constriction in the pipe. Other types of steam meters may include ultrasonic flow meters, thermal mass flow meters, or turbine flow meters, each utilizing different mechanisms to measure steam flow accurately and efficiently.</p></p>
