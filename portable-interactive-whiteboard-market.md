<p><p>Portable interactive whiteboards come in two main market types: front projection and rear projection. Front projection whiteboards project images and interactive content onto the whiteboard surface from the front, making it easier for presenters to see and interact with the content without casting shadows. Rear projection whiteboards, on the other hand, project content onto the whiteboard surface from behind, creating a more seamless and shadow-free presentation experience. Both types offer portability and the ability to create dynamic and engaging presentations.</p></p>
