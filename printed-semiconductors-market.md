<p><strong>The Printed Semiconductors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inkjet</li><li>Gravure</li><li>Screen</li><li>Flexography</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Printed semiconductors can be manufactured using various printing techniques. Inkjet printing involves depositing droplets of ink onto a substrate. Gravure printing uses a printing cylinder with tiny cells containing ink. Screen printing involves forcing ink through a stencil onto the substrate. Flexography is a type of relief printing using flexible plates. Other printing methods include offset and digital printing. Each method has its own advantages and is used depending on the specific requirements of the semiconductor being produced.</p></p>
