<p><p>Electrophysiology technologies and products market can be categorized into three main types: ablation catheters, diagnostic catheters, and others. Ablation catheters are used to treat abnormal heart rhythms by delivering heat or cold energy to destroy small areas of heart tissue. Diagnostic catheters are used to map the heart's electrical activity and identify the source of abnormal rhythms. The "others" category includes products such as mapping systems, EP recording systems, and access devices used in electrophysiology procedures.</p></p>
