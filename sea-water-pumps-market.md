Centrifugal pumps are commonly used for high flow rate applications where water needs to be moved quickly and efficiently. Positive displacement pumps, on the other hand, are preferred for low flow rate applications where precision in pumping is crucial. Both types of pumps play a vital role in various industries such as marine, oil & gas, and desalination plants for transferring sea water efficiently.</p></p>
