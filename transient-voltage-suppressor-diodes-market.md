<p><p>Transient Voltage Suppressor Diodes, commonly known as TVS diodes, can be classified into two main types - Uni-polar TVS Diodes and Bi-polar TVS Diodes. Uni-polar TVS Diodes are designed to protect against positive voltage transients, while Bi-polar TVS Diodes offer protection against both positive and negative voltage transients. Both types of TVS diodes are essential components in modern electronics to safeguard sensitive circuitry and ensure reliable operation by diverting excessive voltage surges away from connected devices.</p></p>
