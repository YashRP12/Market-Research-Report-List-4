Resistive touch screens are durable and cost-effective, suitable for industrial and retail applications. PCAP touch screens provide multi-touch functionality and high durability, making them popular for interactive kiosks and medical devices. Other market types may include infrared touch screens or surface acoustic wave touch screens, offering unique features for specific applications.</p></p>
