<p><strong>The Fiber Cable Termination Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fiber Optic Pigtails</li><li>Fanout Kits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic pigtails are pre-terminated fiber cables with connectors on one end and exposed fibers on the other end. They are used for permanent terminations in fiber optic networks. Fanout kits are used to distribute individual fibers from a multi-fiber cable to separate devices. They provide a reliable and organized solution for terminating a large number of fibers. Both fiber optic pigtails and fanout kits offer efficient and effective termination options for various applications in the fiber cable termination market.</p></p>
