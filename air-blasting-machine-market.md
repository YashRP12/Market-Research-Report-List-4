<p><ul><li>Gravity Suction Type</li><li>Direct Pressure Type</li><li>Blower Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air blasting machines are classified into three main types based on their working mechanism: Gravity Suction Type, Direct Pressure Type, and Blower Type. Gravity Suction Type machines use suction to propel abrasive materials towards the surface to be cleaned. Direct Pressure Type machines rely on high-pressure air to force abrasive material out of a nozzle. Blower Type machines use a high-velocity air stream generated by a blower to propel abrasives. Each type has its own advantages and applications in different industries.</p></p>
