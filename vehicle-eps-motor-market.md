<p><strong>The Vehicle EPS Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS Motor</li><li>REPS Motor</li><li>PEPS Motor</li><li>EHPS Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are four main types of Vehicle EPS Motors in the market:</p><p>1. CEPS Motor: Conventional Electric Power Steering Motor, which is a traditional type of EPS motor used in vehicles.</p><p>2. REPS Motor: Rack-assist Electric Power Steering Motor, which provides additional assistance to the steering rack for smoother steering.</p><p>3. PEPS Motor: Pinion-assist Electric Power Steering Motor, which assists the steering pinion for improved steering control.</p><p>4. EHPS Motor: Electric Hydraulic Power Steering Motor, which uses a combination of electric and hydraulic systems for steering assistance.</p></p>
