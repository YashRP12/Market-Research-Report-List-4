<p><ul><li>Mechanical LiDAR</li><li>Solid State LiDAR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LiDAR for automotive market consists of two main types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR systems use moving parts to scan the environment, offering high resolution but with increased complexity and potential for wear. On the other hand, Solid State LiDAR systems are more compact and reliable as they do not have moving parts, making them suitable for mass production and integration into vehicles. Both types of LiDAR offer different trade-offs in terms of performance, cost, and reliability for automotive applications.</p></p>
