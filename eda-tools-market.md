<p><p>EDA Tools market can be classified into several types based on their functionality and target users. These include Computer-Aided Engineering (CAE) tools, which help in designing and analyzing electronic systems. Printed Circuit Board/Multi-Chip Module (PCB/MCM) tools assist in designing the physical layout of electronic components. IC Physical Design & Verification tools aid in designing and verifying integrated circuits. System-in-Package (SIP) tools are used for integrating multiple electronic components into a single package. Lastly, services market includes providers offering consulting, training, and support services for EDA tools users.</p></p>
