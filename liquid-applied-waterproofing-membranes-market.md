<p><p>Liquid applied waterproofing membranes are widely used in the construction industry to provide protective barriers against water infiltration. The market is segmented into three main types: bituminous, elastomeric, and cementitious membranes. Bituminous membranes are made from bitumen or asphalt, providing excellent waterproofing properties. Elastomeric membranes are flexible and stretchable, adapting to different surface movements. Cementitious membranes are composed of a mixture of cement and polymer-modified materials, offering strong adhesion and durability. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications in the construction industry.</p></p>
