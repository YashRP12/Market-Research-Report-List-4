<p><p>Screen changers are essential equipment in plastic extrusion, filtration, and recycling processes. There are two main types of screen changers, manual and hydraulic. Manual screen changers are operated manually to change the screens, which can be time-consuming and labor-intensive. On the other hand, hydraulic screen changers use hydraulic pressure to quickly and efficiently change the screens, making them more suitable for high-volume production. Both types are important in the plastics industry, but hydraulic screen changers are becoming more popular due to their efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
