<p><ul><li>Static Vortex</li><li>Moving Vortex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor scroll pump market types include static vortex and moving vortex. Static vortex pumps utilize a stationary vortex to create a vacuum or pressure in the system, while moving vortex pumps generate a moving vortex to achieve the same result. Static vortex pumps are known for their simplicity and reliability, making them suitable for low-pressure applications. On the other hand, moving vortex pumps offer higher efficiency and are often used in high-pressure systems where precision control is required.</p></p>
