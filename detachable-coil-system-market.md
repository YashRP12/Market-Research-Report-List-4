<p><p>Detachable coil systems are used in medical procedures such as embolization to block blood vessels. There are three main types of detachable coil systems: Electrolytic Detachable Coil System, Mechanical Detachable Coil System, and Hydraulic Detachable Coil System. The electrolytic system dissolves over time, the mechanical system detaches with a push of a button, and the hydraulic system uses fluid pressure to detach. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in different medical situations based on the needs of the patient.</p></p>
