<p><p>Cardiac stimulators are medical devices used to regulate the heart's rhythm and improve its function. Temporary cardiac stimulators are used on a short-term basis during surgeries or emergencies. Single-chamber and dual-chamber cardiac pacing stimulators regulate the heart rate in patients with bradycardia. Cardioverter defibrillators deliver electrical shocks to restore normal heart rhythm in patients at risk of sudden cardiac arrest. Resynchronized pacing and three-chamber resynchronization automatic cardioversion defibrillators improve heart function in patients with heart failure.</p></p>
