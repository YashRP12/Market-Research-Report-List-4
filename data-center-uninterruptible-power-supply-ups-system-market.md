<p><p>Data center Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) systems come in different types to suit varying needs. Line Interactive UPS systems offer basic protection with automatic voltage regulation. Standby On-Line Hybrid UPS systems provide efficient power protection with fast switchover times. Double Conversion On-Line UPS systems deliver constant and clean power through continuous conversion. Delta Conversion On-Line UPS systems provide the highest level of protection with minimal energy losses. Each market type caters to different requirements and budgets within the data center industry.</p></p>
