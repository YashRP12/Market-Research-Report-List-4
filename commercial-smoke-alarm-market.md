<p><p>Commercial smoke alarms come in three main types: photoelectric smoke alarms, ionization smoke alarms, and dual sensor smoke alarms. Photoelectric smoke alarms are sensitive to smoldering fires, while ionization smoke alarms are more responsive to flaming fires. Dual sensor smoke alarms combine both technologies to provide comprehensive protection against all types of fires. Each type of smoke alarm has its own unique features and advantages, making it important for businesses to carefully consider their specific needs when choosing the best smoke alarm for their commercial space.</p></p>
