<p><p>Aerating cake emulsifiers come in three different types based on their physical form: solid form, liquid form, and powder form. Solid form aerating cake emulsifiers are in solid state and need to be melted before use. Liquid form emulsifiers are ready to use and can be easily added to cake batter. Powder form emulsifiers are in dry powder form and need to be mixed with water before use. Each form offers different benefits and functionalities for aerating cake products.</p></p>
