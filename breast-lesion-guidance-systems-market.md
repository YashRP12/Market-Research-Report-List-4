<p><p>Breast lesion guidance systems market includes various types such as Wire-Guided Localization (WGL), Magnetic Tracers, Electromagnetic Reflectors, and Others. WGL involves the use of a thin wire inserted into the breast to guide surgical removal of the lesion. Magnetic tracers use magnetic seeds to mark the lesion for localization. Electromagnetic reflectors use electromagnetic signals to navigate to the lesion. Other systems may include technologies such as radiofrequency identification or ultrasound for lesion localization during procedures.</p></p>
