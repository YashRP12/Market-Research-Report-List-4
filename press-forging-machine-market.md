<p><p>Press forging machines are used in multiple types of forging processes including closed die forging, open die forging, extrusion, and others. In closed die forging, metal is compressed within a set of dies to achieve a desired shape. Open die forging involves shaping metal between flat dies without enclosing the material completely. Extrusion uses compressive forces to push metal through a die to create a specific shape. Other forging methods may include roll forging, swaging, and upsetting, each with its unique applications in the manufacturing industry.</p></p>
