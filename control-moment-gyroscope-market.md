<p><p>A Control Moment Gyroscope (CMG) is a type of attitude control device used in spacecraft and satellites to accurately orient and stabilize their position in space. <p><p>Control Moment Gyroscope market can be divided into two types - single-gimbal and dual-gimbal. Single-gimbal systems use a single gimbal to control the orientation of the gyroscope, which simplifies the design and reduces costs. On the other hand, dual-gimbal systems use two gimbals to control the orientation, allowing for more precise control and flexibility in maneuvering. Both types are used in various applications such as spacecraft attitude control, stabilization systems, and unmanned aerial vehicles.</p></p>
