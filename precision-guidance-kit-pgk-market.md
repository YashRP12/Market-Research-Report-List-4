There are two main types of PGKs in the market: GPS Guidance Kits and Integrated GPS/INS Guidance Kits. GPS Guidance Kits utilize GPS technology to provide precise location information to the munition, while Integrated GPS/INS Guidance Kits combine GPS and Inertial Navigation System (INS) technology for even greater accuracy. Both types of PGKs are in high demand for military applications due to their ability to improve the effectiveness of guided munitions.</p></p>
