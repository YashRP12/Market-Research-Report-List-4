<p><p>Anesthesia disposables market includes various types such as laryngeal mask airways (LMAs), breathing circuits, endotracheal tubes (ETTs), resuscitators, and anesthesia face masks. LMAs are commonly used for airway management during anesthesia. Breathing circuits deliver gases to patients during anesthesia. ETTs are inserted into the trachea to maintain an open airway. Resuscitators are used for manual ventilation in emergency situations. Anesthesia face masks are used to administer gases to patients during anesthesia. These disposables play a crucial role in ensuring safe and effective anesthesia administration.</p></p>
