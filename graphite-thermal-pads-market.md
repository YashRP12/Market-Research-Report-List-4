<p><p>Graphite thermal pads are available in three main types: Single Layer Graphite Film, Composite Graphite Film, and Multilayer Graphite Film. </p><p>Single Layer Graphite Film consists of a single layer of graphite material with high thermal conductivity. Composite Graphite Film includes additional materials such as copper or aluminum to enhance thermal performance. Multilayer Graphite Film is made up of multiple layers of graphite film to increase thermal conductivity and efficiency. Each type of graphite thermal pad offers unique advantages for different applications in the market.</p></p>
