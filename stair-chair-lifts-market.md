<p><p>Stair chair lifts market is segmented into two main types: straight chair lifts and curved chair lifts. Straight chair lifts are designed for staircases that go straight up without any curves or landings. They are more cost-effective and easier to install compared to curved chair lifts, which are custom-designed to navigate around bends and turns in the staircase. Curved chair lifts are usually more expensive and require a longer lead time for installation due to their customized nature. Both types provide accessibility for individuals with mobility limitations.</p></p>
