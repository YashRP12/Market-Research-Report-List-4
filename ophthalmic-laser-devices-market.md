<p><p>Ophthalmic laser devices market includes various types of lasers such as Diode Lasers, Femtosecond Lasers, Excimer Lasers, Nd:YAG Lasers, Argon Lasers, and SLT Lasers. Each type of laser is used for specific ophthalmic procedures like refractive surgery, cataract surgery, glaucoma treatment, and retinal surgery. Diode lasers are commonly used for retinal photocoagulation, while Femtosecond lasers are preferred for corneal surgeries. Nd:YAG lasers are used for posterior capsulotomy and iridotomy, while SLT lasers are used for glaucoma treatment. Each type of laser offers unique benefits and applications in ophthalmology.</p></p>
