Texturing and cleaning equipment are used for prepping silicon wafers. PECVD equipment is for depositing thin films onto the wafer surface. RPD equipment facilitates the deposition of layers on the wafer. Screen printing equipment is used for patterning and metallization. Other types of equipment in the market include plasma etching systems, laser systems, and inspection tools for quality control in the manufacturing process.</p></p>
