<p><p>Bevel gear reducers are commonly categorized into two main market types: right-angle bevel gearboxes and helical bevel gear reducers. Right-angle bevel gearboxes are designed to change the direction of power transmission by 90 degrees, making them ideal for applications where space is limited or the input and output shafts are perpendicular. On the other hand, helical bevel gear reducers utilize helical gears to provide smoother and quieter operation, making them suitable for applications requiring higher precision and efficiency.</p></p>
