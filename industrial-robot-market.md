<p><p>Industrial robots come in various types, each suited for different applications. Articulated robots have multiple joints for flexibility and reach, ideal for assembly tasks. Parallel robots are known for their high speed and precision, commonly used in pick and place operations. SCARA robots have a vertical articulated arm, perfect for fast and repetitive tasks. Cylindrical robots have a cylindrical work envelope, suitable for welding and painting. Cartesian robots move in straight lines along three axes, offering high accuracy for tasks such as CNC machining.</p></p>
