<p><p>Coffee harvesters market types include Vibration Picking Machines (Derricadeira) and Stripping Machines. Vibration Picking Machines use mechanical vibrations to shake the coffee cherries off the branches, allowing for easier collection. Stripping Machines, on the other hand, strip all the cherries off the branches at once. Both machines help streamline the harvesting process and increase efficiency, saving time and labor costs for coffee producers. Each type of harvester has its own benefits and is chosen based on individual needs and preferences.</p></p>
