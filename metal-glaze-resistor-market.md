<p><p>Metal Glaze Resistor Market includes Thin Film Resistors and Thick Film Resistors. Thin Film Resistors are made by depositing a thin layer of resistive material on a ceramic substrate with superior stability and accuracy, suitable for precision applications. Thick Film Resistors are made by screen printing a resistive paste onto a ceramic substrate, offering lower precision but higher power handling capabilities. Both types of resistors are commonly used in electronic circuits for precise voltage regulation and current limiting.</p></p>
