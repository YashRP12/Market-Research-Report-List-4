<p><ul><li>Concentric Type</li><li>Overlap Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cast resin dry-type transformers are classified into two main types based on the winding arrangements: concentric type and overlap type. Concentric type transformers have windings arranged in a concentric manner, providing excellent short-circuit strength and reliability. On the other hand, overlap type transformers have windings that overlap each other, offering higher short-circuit resistance and improved performance under heavy loads. Both types cater to different requirements and offer unique advantages in terms of efficiency and durability in various applications.</p></p>
