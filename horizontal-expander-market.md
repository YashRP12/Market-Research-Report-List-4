<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Horizontal Expander</li><li>Electric Horizontal Expander</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horizontal Expander market can be categorized into two main types – Hydraulic Horizontal Expander and Electric Horizontal Expander. The Hydraulic Horizontal Expander operates using hydraulic pressure to expand tubes, providing efficient and precise results. On the other hand, the Electric Horizontal Expander utilizes electricity to expand tubes, offering a more versatile and easy-to-use solution. Both types cater to different customer needs and are widely used in industries such as HVAC, automotive, and construction for tube expansion applications.</p></p>
