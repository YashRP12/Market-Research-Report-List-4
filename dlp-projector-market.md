<p><ul><li>1-chip DLP Projectors</li><li>3-chip DLP Projectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DLP projectors are categorized into two main types: 1-chip DLP projectors and 3-chip DLP projectors. </p><p>1-chip DLP projectors use a single chip to create the image by rapidly reflecting light off thousands of tiny mirrors. These projectors are typically more affordable and compact, making them popular for home theaters and classrooms.</p><p>On the other hand, 3-chip DLP projectors use three separate chips for red, green, and blue colors, resulting in higher color accuracy and image quality. These projectors are commonly used in large venues, cinemas, and professional settings.</p></p>
