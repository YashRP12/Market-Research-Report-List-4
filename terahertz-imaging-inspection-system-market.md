<p><ul><li>Passive Terahertz Imaging</li><li>Active Terahertz Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive Terahertz Imaging involves detecting natural terahertz emissions from an object, allowing for non-invasive inspection. This type of market is ideal for applications where minimal disturbance to the object is required, such as in security screening. Whereas Active Terahertz Imaging requires a terahertz source to emit radiation towards the object, providing more control and flexibility in imaging. This type of market is commonly used in industrial settings for quality control and material analysis where a higher level of precision is needed.</p></p>
