<p><p>Explosion protection market includes three primary types: Explosion Venting System, Explosion Isolation System, and Explosion Suppression System. </p><p>1. Explosion Venting System involves releasing the pressure from an explosion to a safe area to prevent damage.</p><p>2. Explosion Isolation System entails isolating the explosion at its source to prevent it from spreading to other areas.</p><p>3. Explosion Suppression System involves detecting and suppressing the explosion before it can cause damage, usually by using chemical agents to extinguish the flames. </p><p>Each system plays a critical role in ensuring safety in potentially hazardous environments.</p></p>
