<p><p>Air Source Heat Pump market consists of two main types - Air-to-Air Heat Pump and Air-to-Water Heat Pump. Air-to-Air Heat Pumps extract heat from the outside air to warm up the indoor air, providing heating and cooling for residential and commercial buildings. On the other hand, Air-to-Water Heat Pumps transfer heat from the outside air to heat water for domestic use or heating system, making them suitable for households and businesses looking for energy-efficient heating solutions. Both types offer eco-friendly and cost-effective heating and cooling options.</p></p>
