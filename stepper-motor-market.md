<p><strong>The Stepper Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Variable-reluctance (VR)</li><li>Permanent Magnet (PM)</li><li>Hybrid (HB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stepper motors are classified into three main types based on their construction: Variable-reluctance (VR), Permanent Magnet (PM), and Hybrid (HB). Variable-reluctance motors have a plain iron rotor and stator with a low holding torque. Permanent Magnet motors have a magnetized rotor with high torque output. Hybrid motors combine features of both VR and PM motors, offering high torque and precision. Each type of stepper motor has its own advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
