<p><p>Active RFID tags market can be categorized into two main types - transponders and beacons. Transponders are RFID tags that actively transmit data to RFID readers, allowing for real-time tracking and monitoring of assets or inventory. On the other hand, beacons are active RFID tags that emit signals periodically, enabling nearby RFID readers to determine the location of the tagged item. Both transponders and beacons play a crucial role in various industries such as retail, healthcare, and logistics for efficient asset management and inventory tracking.</p></p>
